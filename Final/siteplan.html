Site Purpose

The site provides a platform where learners can access materials form their lectures or teachers and are also able to ask questions and teachers are able to give assignments. It majorly acts as a private tutor site.

Scenarios

  1. Where can I find materials easily for my course
  2. How can I interact with my teachers out of class readily for better understanding of concepts
  3. Where can I get my class schedule and assignments digitally
